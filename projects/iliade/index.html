Iliade - Il Gioco

Scopri la storia dei protagonisti dell'Iliade e lanciati nella lotta!

Discover the story of the Iliad's characters and join the epic battle!

My first videogame EVER. I made this as a school project back in 2017, in 9th grade.

The game originally had music taken from other games. Of course, when uploading it online I had to take the music off.

Might work on a remake.

This game is a very heavy PowerPoint presentation: you can't play it online, but you can download it below and run it on your PC. You'll need Microsoft Power Point to play with it, I haven't tested it on other presentation platforms. It might not load if the device is too small. Works best on a computer!

If you have issues with the game, please let me know.
