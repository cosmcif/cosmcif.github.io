Alice and Bob use QKD to plan Eve's surprise party

Help Alice and Bob plan a surprise party for Eve, but make sure she doesn't find out your plans!

Learn the Quantum Key Distribution protocol with this colorful short game.

While previous knowledge of quantum computing can make it easier to understand what's going on in the game, it's not needed at all! You'll be able to understand QKD without background quantum knowledge.
Source: my friends who know nothing about quantum were able to understand QKD with this game.

This game was developed as part of the Womanium Quantum Hackathon 2022. Learn more.
