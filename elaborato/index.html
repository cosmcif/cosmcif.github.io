About the game

I made this game for my Esame di Stato 2020-2021. This is simply a support I used for my exam presentation.

Yes, I could have used a PowerPoint presentation. However, since I started high school with a videogame on a PowerPoint, I wanted to end it with a presentation on a videogame.

My exam topic was on Classic Cryptography and Quantum Computing (RSA vs Shor). If you want to read it, feel free to contact me! But be warned, as it is in Italian only.

This is how my passion for Quantum Computing started. I'm trying to learn as much as I can, hopefully I'll be able to work in that field one day!
